Peaky Blinders

Meet all the Characters and thier famous quots

Tommy Shelby

Thomas Michael ‘Tommy’ Shelby OBE DCM MM MP is the main protagonist of the Peaky Blinders. He the son of Arthur and Mrs. Shelby, brother of Arthur, John, Ada and Finn Shelby, father of Charles and Ruby Shelby, as well as being the husband of Grace and Lizzie Shelby. He is the leader of the Birmingham criminal gang, the Peaky Blinders and the patriarch of the Shelby Family.

"Don't confuse my personality with my attitude. My personality is who I am, my attitude depends on who you are "

Arthur Shelby

Arthur Shelby Jr. is one of the main protagonists of the Peaky Blinders. He is the eldest son of Arthur and Mrs Shelby, older brother of Thomas, John, Ada and Finn Shelby, as well as being the father of Billy Shelby. He was married to Linda Shelby, and is a tough member of the Peaky Blinders. He is also Deputy Vice President of Shelby Company Limited and member of the ICA, acting as his brother's right-hand man. Arthur has drug problem, which get worse over time.

“My Heart Is A Battered Vessel, But Within, There Still Beats A Fluttering Pulse Of A Dream.”

John Shelby

John Michael Shelby (also known as Johnny and John Boy) is one of the two former tritagonists (alongside Polly Gray) of Peaky Blinders. He is a British street gangster who was a soldier during the First World War. He is the third son of Arthur Shelby Sr, brother of Arthur, Thomas, Ada and Finn Shelby, as well as being the husband of Esme Shelby. John has seven children, two sons and two daughters with his deceased first wife Martha Shelby, and three children with Esme, whose genders are unknown. One of his daughters is called Katie Shelby, but his other children’s names are unknown.

“What you call an animal wiht a prick halfway up its back? a cavalry hores.”

Polly Gray

Elizabeth Pollyanna "Polly" Gray (née Shelby) is one of the two former tritagonists (alongside John Shelby) of Peaky Blinders. She is the mother of Michael and Anna Gray, aunt of Arthur, Thomas, John, Ada and Finn Shelby, as well as being the matriarch of the Shelby Family. She is part of the Birmingham criminal gang, the Peaky Blinders, a certified accountant and company treasurer of Shelby Company Limited.

"There’s Only One Thing That Can Blind A Man As Smart As You, Tommy. Love."

Michael Gray

Michael Gray is a major protagonist and later the central antagonist of Peaky Blinders, serving as a supporting character in Season 2, one of the main characters of Season 3 and Season 4, and the central antagonist of both Season 5 and Season 6. He is the son of Polly Gray and the cousin of the Shelby siblings.

"No Matter What It Takes, No Matter How Many Lies I Have To Tell, I Will Take Revenge On Tommy Shelby"

Alfie Solomons

Alfred "Alfie" Solomons Jr. is a Jewish gang leader who resided in Camden Town, London. It is implied that Alfie's mother was a Russian refugee, where his mother was from and he went to school in London with Darby Sabini.

"Intelligence Is A Very Valuable Thing, Innit, My Friend? And It Usually Comes Far Too F***ing Late."

Grace Shelby

Grace Helen Shelby (née Burgess) is an Irish woman who takes up employment as a barmaid at The Garrison Pub, and secretly operates as an undercover agent for the Birmingham Police. Her mission involves getting close to Thomas Shelby in order to retrieve stolen weaponry. She works under Inspector Campbell who used to know her late father, and she claims to be trained as a secret operative.

"May You Be In Heaven A Full Half Hour Before The Devil Knows You're Dead"
